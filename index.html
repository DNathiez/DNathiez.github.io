About Me

My name is Djason Nathiez, student in 3rd year of Game Programming at Rubika Supinfogame, France. I'm a specialized Gameplay Programmer on Unity. I actually work my knowledge in Network Programming because I have a huge interess on network games and the fact that I connect people each others.

My career goal is to become a Gameplay or Network Programmer on games with social aspect. This intention come with the fact that online game took a huge part of my life and make me what I'm today. I'm was a big player of MMORPG (mainly World of Warcraft and DOFUS) and that make my interest in giving opportunities to player to connect each others.

Through my different projects, I developped some skills that I use now as motor of my work style and continue to work on it to improve my capacities.

Teamwork

All my projects was driven by a huge teamwork spirit. Through communication and skill sharing, we worked together to able to achieved the game we worked on. Thanks to that I built solids skills in communication and teamwork, adapt easily on team communication software like Jira, Notion or Trello. I always try to maximise the visibility on my current work with my teammates, looking for feedback in order to make my code strong and in accordance with the request.

Adaptative

Thanks for my multiple experience, I understood that a project may need that I learn new programming methods, languages or software every day. That makes me able to fastly adapt myself to a programming situation and learn faster. It help me to identity a problem, look for multiple solution, choose a way that I can consider as viable, try it to be sure that is good and finally polish it to make it readable, efficient and strong.

Iterative

My opinion is that the development of a video game is never really ended. Sometimes, a Game Designer will come with new features ideas to test, a change of engine or programming architecture can happen or a lot of graphic change may impact the code. In that way, I always tried to make my code iterative and easily modular. That come with readable, independent and structured code. That really helped me on a lot of project which were short in production time and needed fast implementation of feature to test and approve them. A code will never be perfect but I work hard on them to make it easy to iterate on them.

Projects

Sweet Couriers

Sweet Couriers is a Twinstick Shooter where you play plushies in cars that have to drive and shoot inside containers to fill them. Beware, you'll have to play with your teammate to complete this !

On this project, I was assigned as Gameplay Programmer and was in charge of the shooter controller, taking care of fluidity of the feature and adding some accessibility modules as Aim-Assit.

Delight

Delight is a Zelda-like action-adventure game where you play as Kent, a young raccoon searching for his missing father. Wandering through the abandoned Delight Park, you have to face the automatons that have taken over the area and solve puzzles with your cable.

This project was my first 3D experience, developed on Unity, but also the first time I was a full-time programmer on a project. The output of this project was to be a prototype that we had to develop in 4 months.

I was assigned as a Gameplay Programmer to do the game controller, the enemy AI and all the puzzle implementation in the Haunted House, the dungeon, but I also did other secondary tasks like UI, asset integration and sound design.

BeatStrike

BeatStrike is a Rhythm-RPG mobile game developped with Unity. You'll have to play in an endless run, fighting against monster across a mysterious desert surrounded by archeonic ruins.

On this project, I was in charge of gameplay loop and the creation of a tools helping the Game Designer in the creation of pattern composed by sequence of notes. I also worked on UI and integration of assets.

Discosmos

Discosmos is a MOBA where two teams of two players each compete by using the available rails to gain speed in order to unload power. Each team must hit a puck to score in the opponent's camp before the end of the time limit, the team with the most points wins.

This project was the very first online game I created. It was developed in Unity using the Photon Engine SDK to make the network connection. It was a real production challenge because of the time given to make it: 2 months for the whole project.

For this production, I took on the role of Lead Game Programmer and Network Programmer. I was in charge of managing the team of programmers and setting up the whole network infrastructure of the game. I also took care of some small tasks like integrating assets and making UI.

Hard Club Mama

Hard Club Mama is a Roguelike video game where you play an old bigouden annoyed by the sound of the nightclub near of her restaurant. So, she decided to go at the club and bring calm with his Pan.

I came on this project during the production and I mainly did code debugging, user interface programmation, level design and some short task between that like scoring system.
