Delight is a Zelda-like action-adventure game where you play as Kent, a young raccoon searching for his missing father. Wandering through the abandoned Delight Park, you have to face the automatons that have taken over the area and solve puzzles with your cable.

This project was my first 3D experience, developed on Unity, but also the first time I was a full-time programmer on a project. The output of this project was to be a prototype that we had to develop in 4 months.

I was assigned as a Gameplay Programmer to do the game controller, the enemy AI and all the puzzle implementation in the Haunted House, the dungeon, but I also did other secondary tasks like UI, asset integration and sound design.
